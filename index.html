About Us

Baked In Lú is a small Home-Bakery based in Knockbridge, Co. Louth. We bake fresh to order, for supply to fantastic food establishments, retail and leisure/fitness centres across the Wee County. We also produce for our local sustainability focused market: NeighbourFood Blackrock.


We strive for perfection in every bake; sourcing high quality ingredients & packaging as ethically & sustainably as possible. We believe that our passion for food innovation, our keen eye for detail and dedication to the conscious sourcing of raw materials, have helped us to create a USP for our brand and a range of premium Artisan Slices.


Keep an eye out for our aptly titled: ‘Whole-Food Heroes.’ Our ‘HIITMeUpElevenses’ Granola Slices, ‘TrailBlazer Energy Bars,’ ‘PowerUp Pecan Slices’ & ‘fAb Crunch Fuel Bars,’ boast robust nutritional profiles with associated health benefits, which derive solely from whole-food sources. Guaranteed to keep you fuller for longer. Choose to #RefuelTheRightWay #GraduateWithGreatGains and #PowerYourProGoals with Whole-Food Heroes by Beir Bua Bakes. We hope to embrace growth in an organic way with continued gratitude to our current foodie-collaborators and Boyne Valley Flavours for welcoming us into this wonderful community of Producers.
